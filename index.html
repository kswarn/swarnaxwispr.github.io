Why Wispr
Voice is the most natural interface humans have. The challenge of making AI transcription feel invisible, personal, and trustworthy across 110+ apps is the kind of interaction design problem I want to work on. Wispr Flow's "zero-edit output" philosophy resonates with how I think about the quality of AI output which should require as less human intervention as possible.
